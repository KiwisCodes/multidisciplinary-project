Features

Everything you need in a modern bike management app and more

Effortless Bike Oversight

Our Bikey app offers precise bike location tracking on an interactive map. Easily monitor and manage each bike in real-time, view current positions. Experience convenience and peace of mind with Bikey's bike tracking feature!

Track and display detailed bike information in real-time

With our Bikey app, you can effortlessly track and display comprehensive bike details in real-time. Monitor all bikes currently in use, access their current speeds, and receive constant updates on their status. Bikey streamlines bike management, making it simpler and more effective than ever.

Easily monitor bikes within specified zones.

Our new feature ensures bikes stay within designated areas. If a bike strays beyond these boundaries, alerts are triggered. A predefined virtual perimeter is set up initially to define the acceptable limits. This system effectively prevents bikes from exiting the specified zones, maintaining control and enhancing security.
